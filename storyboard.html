Storyboard

Storyboarding really helped me figure out what the goals of my project are, even if I know yet know what the project will be. My first iteration shows a hearing-abled person being curious about ASL and then going online to learn about it and practice it. In the final scene he is able to communicatie with a person from the deaf community. In my second iteration, I wanted to show how ASL can benefit everyone, whether you are hearing-abled or not. For example, communicating from across a room, at a noisy bar or with your infant can all be improved with knowledge of ASL. The guy in the storyboard then starts learning ASL in a way that doesn't disrupt his routine very much but is still effective. In the final scene, he is able to communicate with a wide variety of people in various different situations.
